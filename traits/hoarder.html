Hoarder

Hoarder is a Passive trait that lets a Coromon collect items by chance in battles, even if the Coromon does not participate in the battle. Items are received at the end of battle with a message displaying what item which Coromon found.

The chance that the Hoarder ability triggers at the end of a won battle is 10% per Coromon. Hoarder does not trigger if the player flees. Coromon do not have to participate in the battle for the skill to activate.

The item obtained via Hoarder is random, but it is observed that higher quality items are obtained the higher the level of the Coromon with the trait.
