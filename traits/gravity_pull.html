Gravity Pull

Gravity Pull is a Passive Trait. When in effect, it may prevent the opponent from switching out or fleeing with a 25% chance.
The trait has priority over Escapist, meaning the player Coromon's guarantee of fleeing from a wild Coromon with this ability is effectively nullified.
