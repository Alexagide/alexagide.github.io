Caffeinated

Caffeinated is a Passive Trait that increases the stat growth of a Coromon. The SP growth of all Coromon are equal and bound to the level. Let Y be the level of the Coromon.
Without Caffeinated: 0.3354 Y + 19.64 (green on the graph)
With Caffeinated: 0.4954 Y + 19.44 (bleu on the graph)

Only value lower or equal to 25 are correct, after this level this is extrapolation!
